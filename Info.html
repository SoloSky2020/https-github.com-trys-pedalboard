How the sliders work

The sliders will show up and moving them from left to right will increase that value. Next to all of the the sliders will be a small bar, and that bar will increase if you drag up after clicking on it.



Audio

Plug in your instrument into the computer/phone that you are using. Headphones are highly recommended, but if you don't have any it will work just fine.



Combining effects

There should be a box under the sliders for the effects and there should be a box. After clicking the box it should become blue with a checkmark. That means that it is active.
